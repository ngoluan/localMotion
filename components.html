    Help

    General

    • Why should you get an account?

      Honestly, you probably shouldn't. The only thing it allows you to do right now is the ability to reserve cars in car2go and post messages on the board. If that's useful to you, well, you only need have two things to fill in: email and password.

    • What kind of information do you collect?

      Only what you deliberately provide me. I don't even collect bug reports (although I should). Soon, you'll have the ability to delete your account with all the corresponding information.

    • What do you do when you see a bug?

      Please post it on the board! I'm trying to make this as bug free as possible and am willing to fix every little annoyance or add any useful feature.

    • What's the dashboard?

      I'm hoping that it's all the information that you would need at your fingertips. For TTC, it's the nearest vehicles' predicted arrival times. For BIXI, it's the nearest stations and how many free bikes or empty docks at that station. With car2go, it would be the nearest available car (would be because I haven't implemented this yet).

    • How do I set directions?

      You can either type it in or tap and hold on the map to pick a destination/origin.

    TTC

    • How to see TTC stops?

      Once you zoom into a certain level, TTC stops will appear which you can tap on to get more details

    • How to get vehicle details?

      Just click on a TTC vehicle and details about that vehicle will appear including estimated time of arrival at next stops and location history of that vehicle. Each fading icon represents that vehicle's location at one minute intervals.

    • How to get details about a route?

      Just type in the route number in the search dialogue and click on the desired route.

    • How to give feedback to TTC?

      Click on a vehicle and in the details, you are given the option of providing good or poor feedback to the TTC. Due to technical issues with TTC's own customer service feedback page, your comments will be relayed to TTC's customer service Twitter account at #TTCHelps.

      Note: I would happily give you the option of providing feedback directly (and more discreetly) to the TTC through their customer service page but my request to do that has gone without a response since Summer 2013.

    • How up-to-date is the information?

      The information is provided by NextBus and the City of Toronto (which I am very gracious for) with a maximum lag on my end of one minute.

    BIXI

    • I can't find the station.

      Try clicking on the station, which would show you the details. A StreetView image has been provided to help you find the station.

    • The station isn't on StreetView.

      Try clicking on Open Streetview, which will open the REAL StreetView. Here, you can look around to find the station. If you do, please save it for the next person! If you can't, sometimes BIXI stations move and the StreetView may be outdated. Sorry!

    • LocalMotion said that there were xx amount of bikes/docks at a station and it was wrong!

      I know. This happened to me a couple of times. I get the data right from BIXI and sometimes BIXI lies. I don't know what to do to fix this yet but, thankfully, it's rare.

    car2go

    • How do I book a car?

      Just click on a car on the map and you should be able to book it when the details slide down.

    • What's the “Bingo Fuel” button for?

      Well, did you know that if you fuel up on a car that has less than 25 per cent fuel, you get 20 free car2go minutes? Ever wonder where those low-fueled cars are? Well, click on this button.
